Teaching Interests

I have personal teaching experience in HCI, Databases, Data Structures, Artificial Intelligence, Web Programming, and Introductory Computer Science. I am also very interested in UI/UX and educational technology-related classes.

Education-Related Research

Kokopelli's World (KW) Project | UW CSE Seattle, November 2015 - December 2019

Designed and built an online blocks-based programming learning environment for children with dyslexia. An accessibility project studying how dyslexia affects K-12 children learning to code.

Help Agent for Writing Knowledge (HAWK) Project | UW, January 2011 - September 2016

Designed and developed a suite of web-based lessons to teach reading and writing to learning-disabled K-12 children. An accessibility/education project collaborating with educational psychology department researchers to study reading and writing teaching methods for students with dyslexia and dysgraphia.

CoSolve Project | UW CSE Seattle, May 2009 - April 2016

Worked as lead developer for an online collaborative problem solving and posing environment, a system for users to submit coded problems and/or try to solve them collaboratively with visual support for a history of the problem’s past solving attempts.
